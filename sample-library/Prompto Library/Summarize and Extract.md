Analyze the following content with a focus on {{focus_area}}:

{{selected_text}}

Provide:

**Executive Summary** (3-4 sentences): The core message a busy person needs to know.

**Key Claims** (numbered list): The main arguments or factual claims made, each in one sentence.

**Evidence Quality**: Rate the strength of evidence presented (strong/moderate/weak/missing) and explain why.

**Connections**: What does this connect to in broader discourse? What related ideas does it support or contradict?

**Quotable Highlights**: Extract 2-3 of the most insightful or well-phrased passages verbatim.

**My Take Needed On**: What questions should I form my own opinion about after reading this?
